Our Brief History

Ijaw Brotherhood Empowerment Initiative was officially established on the 25th day of August 2021 with CAC Registration Number RC: 164457
Tax Identification Number: 25267583
SCML from EFCC: 101404045

Ijaw Brotherhood Empowerment Initiative Worldwide was founded by Prince Jack Whiggy Ogei. A bonified son of Ijaw nation. The Brotherhood intends to operate in all cities/towns in Nigeria and Diaspora. It is a well organized Association and has in its fold, seasoned and responsible Ijaw sons and daughters. The Brotherhood is involved in empowering and rendering assistance to its members, the physically challenged, widows/widowers and the society at large by providing humanitarian support and projects to convene love, unity and solidarity worldwide. Ijaw Brotherhood Worldwide is not a secret society or cultic group. It is an association of like-minded Ijaws coming together to assist one another.

Ijaw Brotherhood Empowerment Initiatives (IBEI) is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO), founded by Prince, Jack Whiggy Ogei. Prior to the incorporation of the Brotherhood, the founder was residing in Ughelli North Local Government Area, Ughelli, Delta State. Therefore, it was necessary for the organization to carry Ughelli address to ease the registration process with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). However, during his stay in Ughelli, the founder was impelled by some peculiar circumstances attached to individuals in foreign lands, tribes and cultures etc. thus, soon developed a desire to foster love and unity amongst the Ijaws in these foreign lands. The founder sees solidarity towards one another as one of the most powerful tools of survival in foreign lands.

However, it is the mission of Ijaw Brotherhood Worldwide to cover every town and city in Nigeria and Diaspora to display a hallmark of love amongst its members and the society. The founder believes in the importance of being one’s brother’s keeper through practice of brotherhood based on unity, love, trust and encouragement.

DISCLAIMER

Ijaw Brotherhood Worldwide (IBW) is not a secret society or an occultic group, it is an Association of liked-minded, responsible and reliable Ijaws who came together with Their resources and strength to practice sincere love and to support one another in order to make life better for members and the entire public. Most of its activities are done in public such as ceremonies/parties, inductions, etc, apart from its meetings that are restricted to only members.

Our Mission

1. To practice and maintain a susainable support, love and progress through unity, assistance, solidarity and truthfulness.
2. To create awareness about the Association of ijaw Brotherhood amongst the Ijaw tribe all over the world in order to achieve our aims and objectives.



Our Vision

The founder is aware of the aged long challenges facing the Ijaw people despite Their colossal contribution to the economy of Nigeria by being the owners of the crude oil, which is the bases of Nigeria's wealt and status of afluence asthe giant of Africa.

It is the VISION of the Ijaw Brotherhood to stand in solidarity and raise the standard of living generally through fellowship amongst members in particular, and the whole Ijaw nation where necessary.
